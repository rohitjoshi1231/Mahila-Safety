User Instructions

  • Swipe Up to Call: Quickly initiate a call by swiping up on the screen.
  • Long Press to Share Location: Press and hold to share your current location with others.
  • Create Contact: After adding a contact, tap on their details to make a call.
  • Set Emergency Contact: Hold down on a contact to designate them as an emergency contact.
  • Update Emergency Contact: Easily update your emergency contacts in the settings page.
